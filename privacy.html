Introduction

Welcome to Servitor AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our service, including:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service

  • To manage your account

  • To ensure the security of our service

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase for secure data storage and authentication. Your data is protected by industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data centers

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Specifically:

  • Account information: Retained until you request deletion
  • Manifestation data: Deleted after the specified duration period
  • Payment information: Retained as required by law

5. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have rights including:

  • Right to access your personal data

  • Right to erasure of your data

  • Right to object to processing
