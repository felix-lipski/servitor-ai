Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes!

  • We use Supabase for authentication and storage. It's a very well established and secure service, trusted by the likes of Mozilla and 1Password.
  • The project is open source, you can find the code here.
  • We don't use any third party tracking, analytics or sell your data. We are strictly opposed to how the modern internet works.
  • In case you're still wary, you can always formulate your intentions in a more private way by replacing any sensitive information with nicknames, initials or simple "my master"/"my mistress".
  • You can read our privacy policy for more details.

Do I need to do my manifestations also? Or do I need to forget about it entirely, like with a sigil?

We would like to encourage an active approach. But this is just a tool and a pretty new one at that. We're also only figuring it out. Use it as you see fit. We don't claim to know any one true technique.

Doesn't a servitor usually require a some sort of sacrifice?

Indeed, here the cost is monetary. This sort of computation is also quite energy intensive, on the technical side.

I can just write to a ChatGPT for free every day to the same effect...

On a technical level, this is just a website + cronjob + LLM. Depending on your skill you could set up a personal version of this over a weekend. You would still need to pay for the AI compute or electricity to use it. What we offer is a ready fire-and-forget service at a reasonable rate.
